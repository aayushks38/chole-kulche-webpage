Chole Kulche

My all-time favorite dish!

Delicious, Spicy, and Comforting

About Chole Kulche

Chole Kulche is a classic North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas served with soft, fluffy flatbread called kulche. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients

For Chole

  1. Chickpeas (Kabuli Chana): 1 cup (soaked overnight)
  2. Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  3. Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp
  4. Green Chilies: 2, slit or chopped
  5. Cumin Seeds: 1 tsp
  6. Chole Masala: 2 tbsp (or homemade spice mix)
  7. Salt: To taste
  8. Oil or Ghee: 2-3 tbsp
  9. Water: As needed for gravy
  10. Fresh Coriander: For garnish

For Kulche

  1. All-Purpose Flour (Maida): 2 cups
  2. Baking Powder: 1 tsp
  3. Baking Soda: ¼ tsp
  4. Yogurt (Dahi): ¼ cup
  5. Sugar: 1 tsp
  6. Salt: ½ tsp
  7. Oil or Ghee: 2 tbsp
  8. Water: As needed to knead dough

Quick Recipe

Step 1

Boil chickpeas and prepare the spicy gravy.

Step 2

Prepare the gravy with onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Step 3

Mix spices and cook until aromatic and thick.

Step 4

Serve hot with kulche and garnish with onions and coriander.
